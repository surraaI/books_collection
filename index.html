15 Useful AI Tools – 2025 Edition

🧠 For Productivity & Workflow

  • Notion AI – Smart summaries, writing, and docs.
  • ChatGPT (OpenAI) – Your AI assistant for anything.
  • Claude (Anthropic) – Safe, thoughtful AI assistant.

✍️ For Writing & Content Creation

  • Jasper AI – Marketing copy & blog generator.
  • Copy.ai – Social posts, ads, product text.
  • GrammarlyGO – Tone-aware writing tool.

🎨 For Design & Creativity

  • Canva Magic Studio – Auto-generate designs.
  • Runway ML – Creative video editing AI.
  • Midjourney – Create art from text.

🎤 For Voice, Audio & Video

  • ElevenLabs – Realistic voice generation.
  • Descript – Edit media like a doc.
  • Pictory – Turn blogs/videos to shorts.

💻 For Data, Research & Coding

  • Perplexity AI – AI research with sources.
  • GitHub Copilot – Write code faster.
  • Beautiful.ai – Smart slides & decks.
